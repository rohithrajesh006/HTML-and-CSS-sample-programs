About beaches

A beach is a captivating landscape where land meets the sea, often characterized by sandy or pebbly shores. Beaches are dynamic environments, shaped by the interplay of waves, tides, and wind.They offer a space for recreation, relaxation, and natural beauty, with activities like swimming, sunbathing, building sandcastles, and collecting seashells. The rhythmic sound of waves and the vastness of the ocean create a serene and awe-inspiring atmosphere.
